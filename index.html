PROJECTS

I have worked on several projects including personal and personal. This website is an example of my personal project. Project showcase is below.

"Walking into MHacks 6, our team wanted to stretch our knowledge of machine vision while utilizing Capital One's redesigned API fresh off the stack. Interestingly enough, we came across a philipino twitter account that mockingly retweets others' postings with exposed debit card information under the credo: need a debit card?". and that's how Card Tracker was invented. Card Tracker is an back-end application that crawls and scrape any image at any any base url. Image urls are then sent to a C++ module where we applied Computer Vision technology to detect credit card numbers. These numbers are matched with Capital One API to find and notify compromised accounts. We were a team of 4 people who were distributed into building different components of the app. I mainly worked on the Capital One API where a Python was constructed in which made different types of post and get requests to retrieve datas from their database. Card Tracker won the Best Use of Capital One API". Check out our work at devpost and github !
